## About section draft

Something shifted when I started building software myself.

After years leading strategy, operations, and product-adjacent work at Google, Intuit, and McKinsey, I began using AI tools not just to analyze or write, but to ship products. That changed the kind of work I want to do.

I now build AI-native tools through Paper & Pixel, a small studio focused on helping non-technical people learn, create, and make better high-stakes decisions. In the past year, I have shipped 10+ apps and prototypes across learning, creativity, career navigation, and decision support.

Examples include:

- **StencilCV / Reso** — an AI career application engine that turns one master profile plus a job description into fit analysis, application strategy, tailored CVs, cover letters, and interview prep.
- **Ink Radicals** — a Chinese character learning tool built around semantic and phonetic components rather than rote memorization.
- **ChromaClef** — a playful music composition and notation learning tool.
- **Plinthica** — competition discovery and verification agents for students and families navigating opaque enrichment/admissions ecosystems.
- **Paper & Pixel Playbook** — a guide for non-technical parents to build games with their children using AI.
- **AI-assisted learning games** — small games co-designed with children, exploring how AI can turn imagination into playable systems.

At Google Play & Android, I lead global launches and AI-powered review automation work in a regulated, high-scale environment. I translate complex policy, product, and operational constraints into clear technical requirements, align senior stakeholders, and move cross-functional teams from ambiguity to delivery.

The thread across both my corporate and builder work: I am interested in AI as an execution layer for human judgment, taste, and learning — especially in domains where expert help is expensive, uneven, or inaccessible.

I am open to conversations with teams building AI products in learning, public good, health, creativity, cultural engagement, and decision support.
