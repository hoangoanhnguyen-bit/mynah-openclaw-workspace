# Job Recruiter Screen

Use when Oanh shares a job description, role link, recruiter note, or asks whether a job is worth applying to.

## Core lens

Oanh is not a generic strategy/ops candidate. Position her as:

> Senior Google/McKinsey operator turned AI-native product builder, focused on learning, creativity, public-good products, and high-stakes personal decision tools.

Strong proof points:
- Google / McKinsey / Intuit senior operator background.
- Hands-on AI portfolio: StencilCV/Reso, Plinthica, Ink Radicals, ChromaClef, Paper & Pixel games/playbook.
- Non-technical-by-background but demonstrably builder/product-minded.

## Workflow

1. Identify role category:
   - Strong target: AI learning, EdTech, public good, health, creative AI, internal AI tools.
   - Bridge: agentic workflow / AI transformation with real implementation.
   - Trap: generic strategy/ops, chief of staff, product ops, GTM-only, enterprise change management decks.
2. Separate hard requirements from soft preferences.
3. Flag screen risks:
   - Formal CS/engineering requirement.
   - Years of PM experience in specific domain.
   - Too junior/downlevel.
   - Sales quota / pure GTM / consulting theater.
4. Score fit:
   - Apply hard.
   - Network first.
   - Maybe only if team/comp is exceptional.
   - Skip.
5. Recommend evidence to foreground.
6. If applying, list resume/portfolio angles and likely interview objections.

## Output format

Keep concise.

- **Verdict:** apply hard / network first / maybe / skip
- **Why it fits**
- **Screen risks**
- **How to position Oanh**
- **Best proof points**
- **Next action**
